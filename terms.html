Cancellation & Refund Policy

IMPORTANT NOTICE REGARDING BOOKINGS:

Due to the high demand for our premium instructors and equipment reservation, Marine Tech enforces a strict cancellation policy standard in the nautical tourism industry.

  • No 100% Refunds: Once a reservation is confirmed and paid, we do not offer 100% cash refunds under any circumstance.
  • 50% Refund: Cancellations made 48 hours prior to the activity are eligible for a 50% refund of the deposit or total paid.
  • Rescheduling: We happily allow free rescheduling of your activity (subject to availability and weather conditions) if notified 24 hours in advance.
  • Weather Guarantee: If the activity cannot proceed due to unsafe weather conditions (determined solely by our Naval Certified Instructors), the booking will be rescheduled or credited for future use.

Privacy Policy

Marine Tech respects your privacy. We collect personal information solely for the purpose of securing your booking and ensuring safety protocols (e.g., medical conditions relevant to diving or kitesurfing). We do not sell your data to third parties.

Liability Waiver

By booking any service with Marine Tech (Kitesurf, SUP, Foil, Downwind), the client acknowledges the inherent risks of extreme water sports. While we adhere to strict naval safety standards, clients participate at their own risk and must follow all instructor directives.
