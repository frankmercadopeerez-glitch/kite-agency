Who We Are

Built on Discipline.
Driven by Passion.

A Team of Professionals

Marine Tech is not just a school; it is a standard. Founded in 2020 by Frank Mercado, a Naval Consultant and former Navy Officer, our agency was born from a clear vision: to elevate water sports tourism in Cartagena.

While Frank leads the strategic vision, Marine Tech is a team effort. We are a collective of IKO-certified instructors, professional athletes, and maritime safety experts. We saw visitors receiving subpar experiences with questionable safety measures, and we knew we could do better.

We combine high-performance equipment with military-grade planning. Whether you are kitesurfing, diving, or exploring the islands, you are backed by a team that understands the ocean's power and respects it as much as you do.
