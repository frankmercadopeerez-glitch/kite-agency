FAQ & SUPPORT

Common questions about Kitesurfing in Cartagena.

When is the windy season in Cartagena?

The primary windy season (Trade Winds) runs from December to April, offering consistent 15-25 knot winds. A secondary "Veranillo" season occurs in July and August.

Is Kitesurfing in Cartagena safe for beginners?

Yes. We primarily teach at La Boquilla, which features a wide sandy beach free of rocks and reefs. Our instructors are Naval Certified for maximum safety.

Do I need to book in advance?

Yes. Cartagena is a popular destination. A deposit is required to secure your certified instructor and premium gear (Duotone/Cabrinha) for your specific dates.

What happens if there is no wind?

If conditions are not suitable for riding, we can reschedule your lesson or offer alternative activities like Foil boarding or SUP tours.
